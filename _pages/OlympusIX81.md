== Olympus IX81/ BX61 adapter ==

<table cellspacing=3><tr><td>
'''Summary:'''</td><td valign="top">Controls Olympus IX81/ BX61 microscope</td></tr>
<tr><td>'''Author:'''</td><td>Jeff Kuhn and Arthur Edelstein, BX61 support added by Shalin Mehta</td></tr>
<tr><td>'''License:'''</td><td>not Open Source</td></tr> 
<tr><td>'''Platforms:'''</td><td>Windows, Mac OS X (through serial port)</td></tr>
<tr><td>'''Available since version:'''</td><td>1.3.22</td>

<tr><td valign=top>'''Default serial port settings:'''</td><td valign=top>

{| cellspacing="2" border="0" cellpadding=3
|-AnswerTimeout||500
|-
|BaudRate||19200
|-
|DelayBetweenCharsMs||0
|-
|Handshaking||Off
|-
|Parity||Even
|-
|StopBits||2
|}

</td>
</tr>
</table>
<br>
These serial port settings require all DIP switches on the front of the "Frame Control Unit" (the box connected to the serial cable) to be in their default position: off (zero).

BX61 specific notes: Currently only one filter wheel is supported (connected to FW1 port on the controller).
